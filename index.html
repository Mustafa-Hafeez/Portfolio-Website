- - - About Me - - -

Hi! My name is Mustafa and I am currently a third-year undergraduate specializing in the Entrepreneurship Stream of Computer Science at the University of Toronto. Aside from my passion for computers, I decided to pursue a minor in Natural Sciences & Environmental Management to gain a better understanding of the world around me and to learn more than what is considered to be within the realm of computer science. I am keen to learn new processes & love working to figure out innovative solutions for problems of all sorts.

Previously, I have worked as an Associate Cloud Architect where I was exposed to Cloud Computing Services such as Google Cloud Platform. In just under 4 months, I was able to reconstruct a fully automated branch of software that Tangerine Bank had previously written on a different platform to be transferred over and replicated into GCP in a matter of minutes.

Currently, at the University of Toronto, I am a teaching assistant in the Computer Science, Mathematics & Statistics department. This winter semester, I am TAing CSCA48 (Introduction to Computer Science II) which is taught in the C programming language. Currently, I am an executive of WiCSM - Women in Computer Science, Statistics and Mathematics. I hold the role of Corporate Associate where I assist the team in planning and running events throughout the year.

This is the first functional written Website that I have made from scratch. It was an interesting journey, learning the basics of CSS, HTML & JavaScript. I look forward to developing more websites/apps in the near future.
